Prices: From US$997

Date: October 27 & 28

Website: State of Search

Speakers: Carolyn Shelby (Yoast), Greg Gifford (SearchLab), Duane Forrester (UnboundAnswers), Ryan Jones (Razorfish)

Expect a tight, single-track experience that balances advanced organic strategy with paid and analytics, delivered by industry veterans like Carolyn Shelby and Greg Gifford.

Location: Barcelona, Spain

Prices: From €155

Date: November 13

Website: International Search Summit

Managing international websites and running global campaigns is tricky business. That’s exactly what ISS has built its conference around: how to position your brand in global markets. Its 2025 agenda isn’t out yet, but if 2024’s edition was any indication, you can expect talks across three talks: international seo, international content, and audience & paid.
