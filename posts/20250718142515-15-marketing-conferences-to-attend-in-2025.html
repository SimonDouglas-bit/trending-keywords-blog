Date: October 6-8; November 6

Website: B2B Summit

Speakers: Kate Mackie (EY), Paul Ferron (Forrester), George Colony (Forrester); TBA (B2B Summit APAC)

Forrester’s B2B Summit is the ultimate deep dive into the complex world of business-to-business marketing. What makes this conference special is its data-driven approach: everything is backed by Forrester’s extensive research and real-world case studies.

You’ll explore customer experience optimization, account-based marketing strategies, sales and marketing alignment, and the latest in B2B buyer behavior research. It’s where B2B marketers go to get serious about turning prospects into customers.

Location: Las Vegas, USA

Date: October 15-16

Website: B2B Marketing Expo

B2B Marketing Expo is completely free, which means you can focus your budget on actually implementing what you learn instead of just attending.

This expo combines the energy of a trade show with the insights of a conference, offering a unique mix of exhibitor demonstrations and expert-led sessions.

The focus is on practical, actionable strategies for lead generation, marketing automation, content marketing, and sales enablement that you can start using immediately.

Location: San Francisco, USA

Prices: From US$1,499

Date: October 14-16

Website: Dreamforce

Speakers: Ellen Pompeo (Actress & Producer), Jesse Eisenberg (Actor & Filmmaker), Rob Lowe (Actor & Director), Andrew Ng (Coursera, DeepLearning.ai), Tekedra Mawakana (Waymo)

Hosted by the OG SaaS company Salesforce, one of the biggest in the world, Dreamforce is a full-scale marketing and technology extravaganza that takes over San Francisco every fall.

What sets it apart is the incredible mix of A-list entertainment, cutting-edge tech demos, and seriously useful marketing insights all rolled into one massive event.

You’ll dive into customer relationship management, marketing automation, AI-powered personalization, and the future of customer experience, all while being entertained by Hollywood stars and inspired by industry visionaries.

Location: Dallas, USA

Prices: From US$895

Date: October 7-9

Website: VidSummit

VidSummit is where video marketing gets serious and seriously fun.

With MrBeast and other YouTube legends sharing their secrets, this conference is all about mastering the art and science of video content creation.

What makes it unique is the perfect blend of creative storytelling techniques and data-driven growth strategies.

You’ll learn about video SEO, audience engagement tactics, monetization strategies, and the latest trends in video marketing across all platforms.

Location: Singapore

Prices: From SG$799

Date: September 24-25

Website: Digital Marketing Asia

Speakers: Neil Patel (NP Digital), Rezwana Manjur (Marketing-Interactive), Kok Pooi-san (Wingstop), Caitlin Nguyen (Abbott), Tay Yan Long (Changi Airport Group)

Digital Marketing Asia is your gateway to understanding the rapidly evolving Asian digital landscape.

What makes this conference special is its focus on the unique challenges and opportunities of marketing in Asian markets, where consumer behavior and digital platforms can be vastly different from Western markets.

You’ll explore mobile-first marketing strategies, social commerce trends, influencer marketing in Asia, and how to navigate the diverse digital ecosystems across different Asian countries.

Prices: From £795

Date: September 18-19

Website: SMX London

SMX London is where search marketing professionals go to stay ahead of the constantly changing SEO and PPC landscape.

SMX is tactical and hands-on. It isn’t just about theory; you’ll get actionable strategies you can implement immediately. The focus is on advanced SEO techniques, paid search optimization, local search strategies, and how to adapt to the latest algorithm changes and industry updates.

Advanced Facebook and Google Ads tactics, conversion rate optimization, landing page design, and the latest in programmatic advertising—all with a European perspective that you won’t find elsewhere
