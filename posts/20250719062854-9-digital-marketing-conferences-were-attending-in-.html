Dates: September 3-5

Location: San Francisco, USA

Speakers: Amy Poehler, Marques Brownlee, Dario Amodei, Dharmesh Shah

Prices: from US$799

Website: https://www.inbound.com/

Hosted by the marketing OGs HubSpot, INBOUND is a massive marketing conference offering hundreds of talks, deep dives, and fireside chats about topics ranging from brand strategy to AI.

Watch their recap video here:

One of INBOUND’s pull is the fact they get some of the biggest names on stage as their speakers. Last time round, they even got former U.S President Barack Obama as one of their speakers.

This time round, the big names include actress Amy Poehler, Anthropic CEO Dario Amodei, YouTuber Marques Brownlee, and the host of Hot Ones, Sean Evans.

Dates: September 23-24

Location: San Diego, USA

Speakers: Britney Muller, Alex Halliday, Ross Hudgens

Prices: from US$615

Website: https://us.brightonseo.com/

BrightonSEO is back for its third year in the US, and there are already some pretty impressive speakers and talks announced.

A few ones to look out for:

Dates: October 14-15

Location: San Diego, USA

Speakers: Madhav Bhandari, Mark Schaefer, Kevin Indig, Emily Kramer, Tim Soulo, Patrick Stox, Ryan Law

Prices: from US$799

Website: https://ahrefsevolve.com/

Last year, we launched our first ever SEO and digital marketing conference, Ahrefs Evolve in sunny Singapore. Watch our recap video:

This year, we’re bringing it to the US, and in sunny San Diego! And this year, it won’t just be an SEO conference; we’re going bigger and better. It will be a 600-person marketing conference with the best minds in marketing, all gathering to network, catch up, and discuss the latest and greatest in marketing strategy and tactics.

And if you opt for a VIP ticket, you’ll even get a private yacht networking event with the Ahrefs team and all the invited speakers. Pretty cool, huh?

Dates: September 18-21

Location: Shenzhen, China

Speakers: Gary Illyes, Aleyda Solis, Jessica Xiao, Cristina Song, Ge Fei

Prices: from US$799

Website: https://shenzhenseoconference.com/

Hosted this year at The Ritz-Carlton Shenzhen, this four-day gathering pitches itself as a bridge between Eastern and Western search communities.

Expect 40-plus heavyweight speakers—including Google’s Gary Illyes and international consultant Aleyda Solis—alongside hands-on workshops, city tours and high-level networking designed to help brands crack the Chinese market while staying plugged into global best practice.

Dates: September 26

Location: Cluj-Napoca, Romania

Speakers: Andrei Țiț, Alex Nigmatulin, Bibi the link builder

Prices: from €120

Website: https://www.searchevolutionsummit.com/

Billed under the banner “Search Beyond Limits,” this one-day summit in Romania’s tech capital mixes eight tactical sessions with optional advanced workshops for just €120.

Talks from our very own Andrei Țiț, Alex Nigmatulin and “Bibi the Link Builder” focus on data-backed SEO, AI-powered content and link-building that actually moves the SERP needle, while the organisers promise an “all-hats” view—white, grey and black—of what’s working right now.

Dates: September 3-5

Location: Shiga Prefecture, Japan

Website: https://brand.summit-japan.com/

Set against the calm backdrop of Lake Biwa, Brand Summit is a free, invite-only retreat for senior Japanese and Asia-Pacific marketers.

Sessions dig into “marketing leadership,” asking how brands can harness AI, storytelling and community-building to thrive in an era of constant upheaval.

The emphasis is on peer-to-peer discussion rather than exhibition-hall bustle, making it ideal if you want deep conversations and a reset away from Tokyo’s glare.

Dates: October 9

Location: Chandigarh, India

Speakers: Mads Singers, Alan Thomas, Steve Wiideman, Navneet Kaushal

Prices: from ₹9,250

Website: https://amazeconference.com/

A single-day, 100 % SEO conference at the Hyatt Regency Chandigarh, Amaze pulls 300+ practitioners for rapid-fire, case-study-driven talks from the likes of Mads Singers, Alan Thomas and local stalwart Navneet Kaushal.

The agenda revolves around AI, automation and “no-theory” playbooks, promising attendees they’ll leave with tools, templates and a head-full of tactics they can deploy the very next day.

Dates: October 27-28

Location: Grapevine, USA

Speakers: Carolyn Shelby, Greg Gifford, Duane Forrester, Ryan Jones

Prices: from US$997

Website: https://www.stateofsearch.org/conference-2025/

Presented by DFWSEM, State of Search positions itself as the “Who’s Who of What’s What” in digital marketing.

Expect a tight, single-track experience that balances advanced organic strategy with paid and analytics, delivered by industry veterans like Carolyn Shelby and Greg Gifford.

Dates: October 23-24

Location: Brighton, UK

Speakers: Dixon Jones, Luke Carthy, Erin Simmons

Prices: from £330

Website: https://brightonseo.com/

It’s no surprise that BrightonSEO made our list: The biannual conference is well-loved by SEOs and marketers of all levels, ourselves included.
