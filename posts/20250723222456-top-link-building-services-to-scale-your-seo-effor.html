Link building continues to play a vital role in SEO. While search engines have made significant advancements in understanding content through machine learning, links remain a core signal for ranking websites. Google’s official stance encourages earning links naturally through valuable content.  However, in practice, most businesses, whether large brands or local SMBs, require visibility in search results to grow. That visibility often begins with a nudge that link building can provide. A well‑earned backlink profile amplifies a page’s visibility, and that visibility, not the links themselves, sparks a chain reaction of new mentions.  An Ahrefs study tracking 10 ,000 keywords showed that pages already ranking in Position 1 picked up new referring domains 5‑14 % faster each month than lower‑ranking URLs, evidence that search exposure drives additional links rather than vice‑versa. As more people see and trust those high‑ranking pages, journalists and bloggers naturally reference them, reinforcing a self‑perpetuating flywheel. Early research on generative‑AI surfaces suggests a similar pattern. Seer Interactive found that 87 % of ChatGPT’s SearchGPT citations matched Bing’s top organic results, indicating that pages with strong traditional SEO signals, including robust link authority are more likely to be quoted by AI assistants. 

Likewise, a 2025 SE Ranking study of Google AI Overviews reported that the feature “tends to cite websites with domain authority … and solid backlink profiles.”

links aren’t the only factor in either scenario, but the evidence shows they remain a gateway to the visibility, human and machine that drives ongoing discovery and trust. Despite its importance, link building remains a time-intensive and specialized task. For companies without existing publisher relationships or in-house outreach capabilities, link building services offer a practical path forward. Done right, these services connect your content with relevant publishers and help build links that improve visibility, traffic, and domain authority. Link building is still mission‑critical, but the winning formula is precision over sheer volume. Google evaluates context, relevance, and source quality, folding them into broader E‑E‑A‑T judgments, so only placements that genuinely add value will move the needle. Preliminary research also shows that pages already seen as authoritative in organic search are more likely to appear in generative AI answer boxes, hinting that high‑quality links can amplify discoverability across both classic search and emerging AI channels, though links are just one piece of the puzzle. Whether you rely on a full‑service agency for turnkey efficiency or commission a handful of niche‑relevant placements to deepen topical expertise, choose partners whose methods align with your brand’s risk tolerance, reporting needs, and long‑term visibility goals.
