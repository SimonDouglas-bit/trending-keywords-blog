By anchoring Pay Per Crawl in a clear purpose – fair pricing for AI crawlers – Cloudflare showed that campaigns rooted in genuine brand values resonate more deeply than purely tactical SEO plays.

This purpose-driven approach sustained visibility across any channel, building trust and momentum even before any revenue was generated. 

Drawing on Simon Sinek’s concept “Start With Why”, Cloudflare’s “Content Independence Day” exemplified leading with purpose rather than product features.

It articulated why the initiative mattered (protecting publisher rights) before detailing how it worked. 

This differentiated the campaign and cemented the brand’s authority in both search results and AI-driven responses.
